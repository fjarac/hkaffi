Um okkur / About us

Halldórskaffi er vinalegur veitingastaður í elsta hluta Víkurþorps, í sögufræga húsinu Brydebúð.

Halldórskaffi - Restaurant / Cafe / Bar is located on the ground floor of an historic house called Bryde‘s Store (Brydebúð). It has an old-fashioned atmosphere and we offer a variety of refreshments.

Umsagnir / Reviews

Great food and service, perfect for large groups

This restaurant has a very homey feeling, attentive service, and very good food. We really loved the local char! This is a great option for dinner, especially for groups.

So Great the 1st Time, HAD to Go Back Again!

We so enjoyed this "hole in the wall" quaint place for lunch on our way through Vik from the Golden Circle on our way East to the Glaciers. Their pizza with cheese and DATES was delicious. We loved it so much that we made a point to go again on our way back west a few days later. A great value and really great pizza!!

May have been our best meal in Iceland.

Exhausted and somewhat stinky from horse riding, we were welcomed into the cozy restaurant for a delicious dinner. We had the artic char and the lamb. Both were very tasty and the little mushroom souffle side that came with the lamb was heavenly. Then dessert with meringue, mint, chocolate and cream was just the perfect end to the dinner.

Great Spot!

The food was delicious especially the Artic Char and Salad with Dilled Salmon. The portions were a nice size and the waitress was great. From the appetizers to the entrees to the dessert everything was delicious. This is a great place to stop in Vik. We even asked our waitress about the Aurora Borealis and everything she was perfect. Right as we stepped outside of the restaurant they were right there. Such an amazing experience.
